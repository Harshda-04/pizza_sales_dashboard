# 🍕 Pizza Sales Dashboard - Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Pizza Sales Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze sales performance, customer ordering behavior, and product trends.

The dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable business insights through KPIs, trend analysis, category breakdowns, and best/worst seller identification.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Monitor overall sales performance
- Analyze customer ordering patterns
- Identify top and bottom-performing pizzas
- Track revenue and order trends
- Understand category-wise and size-wise sales distribution
- Support data-driven business decisions

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Power Query
- Data Modeling
- Data Visualization

## 📈 Business Insights

### Customer Behavior

- Most orders occur during weekends.
- Friday and Saturday evenings show peak demand.
- Customers purchase an average of 2.32 pizzas per order.

### Revenue Analysis

- Total revenue exceeded $817K.
- Premium pizzas contribute significantly to revenue generation.

### Category Performance

- Classic category contributes the highest sales and orders.
- Chicken and Veggie categories also show strong performance.

### Size Analysis

- Large-size pizzas generate the highest share of sales.
- Medium-size pizzas are the second most preferred option.

### Seasonal Trends

- Highest order volumes observed during July and January.
- Demand remains relatively consistent throughout the year.

  ## 🏢 Business Problem

Pizza restaurants generate thousands of sales transactions every year. However, raw sales data alone does not provide clear answers to important business questions such as:

- Which pizzas generate the highest revenue?
- Which products are underperforming?
- During which days and months is demand highest?
- Which pizza category contributes the most to sales?
- Which pizza sizes are preferred by customers?
- How can inventory and staffing be optimized based on sales trends?

Without proper analysis, management may struggle to make informed decisions regarding inventory planning, marketing campaigns, menu optimization, and resource allocation.

---

## 💡 Solution

To address these challenges, an interactive Power BI dashboard was developed that converts raw sales data into meaningful business insights.

The dashboard provides:

- Real-time KPI tracking
- Best and worst seller analysis
- Daily and monthly sales trends
- Category-wise performance analysis
- Size-wise sales distribution
- Revenue and order pattern visualization

These insights help stakeholders quickly identify opportunities and make data-driven business decisions.

---

## 📈 Key Insights Derived from the Dashboard

### 1. Revenue Performance
- Total Revenue generated: **$817.86K**
- Average Order Value: **$38.31**

**Business Impact:**
The business is generating strong revenue per transaction, indicating healthy customer spending behavior.

---

### 2. Customer Ordering Behavior
- Total Orders: **21,350**
- Total Pizzas Sold: **49,574**
- Average Pizzas per Order: **2.32**

**Business Impact:**
Customers typically purchase more than two pizzas per order, suggesting opportunities for combo offers and upselling strategies.

---

### 3. Peak Demand Analysis
- Highest order volume occurs on **Fridays and Saturdays**
- Peak months include **July and January**

**Business Impact:**
Management can increase staffing, inventory, and promotional activities during high-demand periods to maximize sales.

---

### 4. Best-Selling Products
- Thai Chicken Pizza generates the highest revenue.
- Classic Deluxe Pizza leads in quantity sold and total orders.

**Business Impact:**
These products can be highlighted in marketing campaigns and promotional offers to further boost sales.

---

### 5. Underperforming Products
- Brie Carre Pizza consistently ranks among the lowest in revenue, quantity, and orders.

**Business Impact:**
Management can reconsider pricing, marketing strategy, or menu placement for these products.

---

### 6. Category Performance
- Classic Pizza Category contributes the highest sales and orders.

**Business Impact:**
The Classic category represents the core customer preference and should remain a strategic focus.

---

### 7. Pizza Size Preference
- Large pizzas account for the largest share of sales.

**Business Impact:**
Inventory planning should prioritize ingredients and packaging associated with large-size pizzas.

---
